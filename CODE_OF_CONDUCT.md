# Code of conduct

## Our standard

We want a technical community where people can question assumptions, report
problems, and disagree about designs without personal hostility.

Expected behavior includes respectful critique, evidence-based discussion,
clear attribution, patience with different experience levels, and care for the
people affected by software decisions.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized
attention, doxxing, deliberate deception, publishing private information, or
sustained disruption of project spaces.

## Scope and enforcement

This policy applies in repository issues, discussions, pull requests, reviews,
and project events. Maintainers may edit or remove violating content and may
warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently ban participants according to the
severity and pattern of behavior.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's
[private reporting channel](https://github.com/kalibraring/agent-tree-rl/security/advisories/new)
and label the report as a conduct concern. The maintainer will protect reporter
privacy as far as reasonably possible and will disclose conflicts of interest.
